Universities and invention advisors

Supporting invention advisors at the next step

When an invention disclosure reaches your university, the next decisions shape what happens after. BayPAT works with invention advisors to assess inventions, protect them where appropriate, and open the right route into commercialization and transfer.

Discover moreGet in contact

IP and patenting

When an invention disclosure is received, the first need is clarity. BayPAT supports invention advisors with evaluation, patenting strategy, and IP-related decisions that help determine what should move forward and how.

Invention evaluation

BayPAT assesses invention disclosures from the perspective of both patentability and market relevance. The result is a stronger basis for deciding whether the invention should be taken forward and whether a patent application is advisable.

Patenting strategy

In coordination with the university and the inventors, BayPAT develops the route into patenting: from strategic positioning and filing logic to support throughout the patenting process.

IP scouting

For universities that want to make IP more visible internally, BayPAT can support a more active approach to identifying inventions and strengthening awareness around transfer potential.

Ask for a consultation

Commercialization

Once an invention is ready to move forward, the focus shifts to the route into application. BayPAT supports partner search, spin-outs, agreements, and the steps that connect protected IP with the right commercial setting.

Partner search and marketing

The route into industry depends on finding the right partner. BayPAT draws on its network and commercialization tools to identify suitable companies and move technologies toward licensing and application.

Spin-offs

If inventors are planning a company, the commercialization route may lead through a spin-out rather than a licensing-only path. BayPAT supports the questions that arise around that transition and the transfer of university-based IP into a new venture.

Contracts and negotiations

Commercialization often depends on getting the contractual framework right. BayPAT supports negotiation and develops agreements tailored to the invention, the partner, and the transfer route.

Contract controlling

After a partner has been found and an agreement is in place, BayPAT can continue to support the ongoing coordination and controlling of that contractual relationship.

Ask for a consultation
