For Industry

Finding relevant technologies from research

BayPAT connects companies with technologies emerging from Bavarian universities and research institutes. That gives industry a clearer route into licensing, partnerships, and commercially relevant opportunities.

Get in touchBrowse technology offers
Editorial imageBayPAT gives companies access to technologies from Bavarian universities and research institutes, with clear routes into licensing and partnership.

What BayPAT offers companies

BayPAT helps companies identify relevant technologies from Bavarian research and move them toward evaluation, licensing, and collaboration. The offer is designed to make that route easier to understand and easier to act on.

Find relevant technologies faster

BayPAT brings together technologies from 28 Bavarian universities, universities of applied sciences, and research institutes. That gives companies a more direct route to research-based opportunities that may otherwise be difficult to find.

Talk to one partner, not twenty

Companies do not need to navigate multiple institutions on their own. BayPAT provides one central contact to help identify relevant technologies and coordinate the next step.

Move from first interest to a workable deal

BayPAT supports the route from initial interest to the next commercial conversation. That can include evaluation, licensing discussions, and the transfer steps needed to move a technology forward.

Evaluate opportunities with stronger IP backing

BayPAT focuses on technologies with a strong IP basis and professionally supported transfer routes. That gives companies a more reliable starting point for evaluation, licensing, and partnership decisions.
